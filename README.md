# EU-Food-Rejection-Analysis
This project is an analysis of rejected food products data from EU member countries for HICI-  a local food export company.  The goal was to identify high-risk products, their countries of origin, and enforcement actions taken.

 Business Questions
- Which EU member reports the highest number of food rejections?
- Which country of origin has the most rejected products?
- What products are most frequently rejected?
- What actions are most commonly taken?


## 🛠 Tools Used
- Excel (data cleaning & analysis)
- PostgreSQL (data analysis)
- Power BI (visualization)

---

 Key Insights

- Nuts and nut-based products drive the highest volume of rejections  
- Meat and meat products (excluding poultry) present the greatest risk, with the highest severity rate  
- Re-dispatch is the most common enforcement action  
- Risk is concentrated in specific product categories rather than evenly distributed
