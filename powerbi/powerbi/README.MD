##Power BI Dashboard

An interactive dashboard was developed to analyze EU food rejection data and identify high-risk products, countries of origin, and enforcement patterns.

---

### Dashboard Features

- **KPI Overview**
  - Total incidents  
  - Severe cases  
  - Severity rate  

- **Top Reporting EU Member States**
  - Identifies countries reporting the highest number of food rejections  

- **Country of Origin Analysis**
  - Highlights exporting countries with the highest rejection rates  

- **Product Category Analysis**
  - Shows the most frequently rejected food products  

- **Severity Analysis**
  - Measures the proportion of severe enforcement actions by product category  
  - Distinguishes between high-frequency and high-risk products  

- **Action Taken Breakdown**
  - Displays the most common enforcement actions (e.g., re-dispatch, destruction)

- **Interactive Slicers**
  - Filter by product category, country of origin, and severity group  

---

### Key Insights

- Nuts and nut-based products account for the highest number of rejections  
- Meat and meat products (excluding poultry) exhibit the highest severity rate  
- Re-dispatch is the most frequently applied enforcement action  
- Risk is concentrated in specific product categories rather than evenly distributed
- Low risk product categories include cocoa and cocoa products, fruits and vegetables and crustaceans

---

### Design Approach

- Used a clean, structured layout to prioritize key business questions  
- Applied consistent color coding:
  - Blue → general metrics  
  - Deep red → severity and risk  
- Incorporated slicers for interactive exploration  
- Balanced detail and readability to support decision-making  

---

### Outcome

The dashboard enables quick identification of:
- High-risk product categories  
- Key exporting countries  
- Enforcement patterns across the EU  

It supports data-driven decision-making in food safety monitoring and risk management.
